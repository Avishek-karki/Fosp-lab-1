KHWOPA ENGINEERING COLLEGE

Affiliated to Purbanchal University,Estd 2001
(Dedicated to Country and People)



KheC is Nepal's first community-based engineering college,undertaken by Bhaktapur Municipality.

People of Bhaktapur had a dream of at least one graduate per family and ultimately intellectualizing the whole society. To realize this dream Khwopa College, Khwopa Engineering College, Khwopa College of Engineering, Khwopa Secondary School and Khwopa Polytechnic Institute were established.
